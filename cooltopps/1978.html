1978 Topps

#36 Eddie Murray

In 1978 I only bought about 5 packs of cards. So, 10+ years later when I started to try to complete all my topps sets, I was thrilled to discover that this was one of the cards I pulled in those 5 packs! I always liked this picture because it just looks like Eddie is thinking... "I'm going to hit 500 home runs and be a pain in the media's collective butt while I do it!". Another favorite of mine, the #200 Reggie, is also a classic. That is just the pose you think of when you think of Reggie.
