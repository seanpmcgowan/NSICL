1972 Topps

#309 Roberto Clemente

For 1972 Topps took the "action" out of the regular cards and put in a bunch of "in action" cards, mostly of the big stars. The #434 Johnny Bench card is my favorite one. For the "regular" cards, this Clemente has always been one of my favorites. The #19 Billy Cowan card begs the question..."did they do that on purpose?"
