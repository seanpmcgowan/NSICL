1985 Topps

#65 Bill Buckner

In 1985 Topps had a bunch of special cards... record breakers, all-stars, father-and-son cards... but, the standard cards just didn't have much for cool cards. They looked sort of flat. This one is a throw-back. Doesn't it remind you of the '61 Mantle? A classic shot of a great player.
