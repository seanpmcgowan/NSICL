2021 Topps

#255 Austin Hays

This is actually kind of a bland year. And I want to assure you that the fact that Austin is wearing an Orioles uniform had nothing to do with my choice... This is just a great shot of a guy playin' ball, and doing it right. A close runner up was #233 Dansby Swanson. This reminded me a myself when I was 16 in the championship game! I don't know what happened here, but in my instance, the catcher missed the ball and there was no play. We still lost though :( Also for your entertainment, we have:

  • some sillyness with Austin Riley #111 and Didi Gregorius #561. The feet on the Riley card are my favorite!
  • a couple nice bunting shots Gregory Polanco #412 and Eddie Rosario #356 both are good but I like Rosario's with the ball on the lower half of the bat!
  • Mauricio Dubon #8 doing his best Sadaharu Oh.
  • Juan Soto #330 and Alex Kirilloff #413 harken back to one of my fav WS cards from the '69 set.
  • diving for a catch is "future star" Trent Grisham #249
  • sliding head first into third very seriously is Josh Harrison #290
  • and finally we have at least the 4th player in a classic pose that started with Rocky Bridges in 1956...Ronald Acuna #263
