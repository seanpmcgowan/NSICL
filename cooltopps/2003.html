2003 Topps

#100 Ichiro Suzuki

Surprisingly, there aren't many cool shots in this set. Perhaps the fact that this is Ichiro tipped my hand a little, but I try to go with the cards that make me say "cool", and who the player is is part of what makes me say that. There was a funny picture, #234 Matt Lawton apparently having a tough time with the sun on this pop-up. Also in one of the substsets, #702 Edmonds is a really cool shot, but not a "regular" card. As I was going through the set, I thought I had found the same card in the wrong spot when I got to #493 Craig Wilson after seeing #391 Bubba Trammell. Funny huh?
