1988 Topps

#443 Ken Griffey

For whatever reason, there weren't many nifty shots here. I went with Griffey working his lead off 2nd base. A nice baseball picture. The '88 set contained "team leaders" cards. Two nifty ones are #51 Orioles (Murray/Ripken) and #759 Athletics (McGwire/Canseco). Two pretty cool cards. I don't know about you, but when I watched the Red Sox in 1988, I thought they had it going. Check out #269 Burks and #493 Greenwell. What happened? Finally, we have an obvious vintage card collector and Mickey Mantle fan...Mike Hart - #69 surely planned this!
