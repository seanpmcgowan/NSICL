1953 Topps

#69 Dick Brodowski

The '53's are all head shots until the last series where there are maybe a dozen or so "action" poses (most notably #244 Mays - mine is trimmed). What makes this set special is the nifty artwork, including the backgrounds. This one is my favorite because it depicts a night game in the background! Pretty cool. Dark sky and all. And, if you read the back (as you always should) the writers call Dick "one of the brightest young prospects". Coincidence? I think not!
