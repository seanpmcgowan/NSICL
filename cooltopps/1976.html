1976 Topps

#230 Carl Yastrzemski

The '76 set is a nice set. They had some "traded" inserts just like in 1974. For my favorite card, I give you this very cool shot of Yaz. The #300 Bench was a close second for me. The '76 set also contains the very unique card #564 Kurt Bevacqua, winning a bubble blowing contest! Only in America, and only in Baseball!
