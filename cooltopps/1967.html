1967 Topps

#600 Brooks Robinson

The '67 design is very similar to the '57 design. Just bigger, more colorful letters. This is perhaps my 2nd favorite set. Again, filled with nice poses of players I like: #5-Ford,#30-Kaline, #100-Frank Robinson,#250-Aaron,#319-Paul Blair and #528-Rico Petrocelli. There are also a couple of cards special to my heart...#1 of Orioles Manager Bauer and his meal tickets Brooks and Frank, and #154 - World Series game 4 card, depicting Brooks about 3 feet in the air running and jumping towards Dave McNally, who just finished off a complete game shutout of the Dodgers to complete the 4 game sweep! Great picture! And by now you may have a clue as to why the card shown is my favorite for this set. :)
