1991 Topps

#455 Walt Weiss

The 1991 set has a ton of good game photos. This one is my favorite. Walt is about a mile from the bag! The funny thing I noticed going through this set is that the editors didn't seem to care how silly they made the players look... check out #657-Kingery, #472-Manwaring, #165 Sandy Alomar, #496 Barrett, #437 Dascenzo, #672 Shane Mack, and #614 Salazar. But, the sillyest looking ones are without a doubt #667 Kent Anderson and #148 Rey Palacios. Finally we have proof that there is a fine line between a cool pose and a head case. You decide about #450 Boggs and #530 Clemens.
