1994 Topps

#149 Kenny Lofton

The '94 set had bunches of nifty shots, but I have always liked this one since I first pulled it from a pack. And you know Kenny gets this ball. There is so much goofy stuff here, I'm just going to make a list...

  • #180 Brett would be more cool, if it weren't a grounder to 2nd (note ball)
  • #675 Roberto Alomar not showing his brother any love.
  • #345 Kevin Brown doing his best Carl Hubbell.
  • #422 Rey Sanchez signing his '93 topps card.
  • Topps trys some special effects on #300 Sandberg and #132 Bret Barberie
  • They also include a nifty commemerative card with #715 (get it?) Aaron
  • Finally, if you aren't playing on picture day #705 Rijo and #511 White have the photo-ops covered.
