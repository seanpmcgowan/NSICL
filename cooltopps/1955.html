1955 Topps

#152 Harry Agganis

The 1955 set is not ugly, but it is pretty redundant. Harry's pose is about the best they could come up with, but what put this card on my list is the fact that we (baseball and the Red Sox) lost Harry so young. He was the perfect home-town (Lynn Ma.) hero for Boston. He would have been a veteran player on the '67 team. Wow.
