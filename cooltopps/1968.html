1968 Topps

#118 Mark Belanger

Although they are a very nice design, the '68s don't do much for me. I do love this card of "the blade". If you never saw this guy in action, then you don't know what "range" is! A couple close contenders were RedSox #140-Tony C. and #250-Yaz. The World Series card for the 1967 series were cool. My favorite one is #152 game 2, staring Yaz. There is also a great insert set in 1968 that I use to play simulated games with my son. I don't know how much research they did to try to make the resluts work out reasonable, but they usually do. Try it if you get a chance. My favorite card from the 33 card set is the #8 Home Run card, of the "Say Hey" kid, of course!
