1999 Topps

#100 Ken Griffey

Just a great baseball picture, and a classic lefty swing. #265 Nomar is also kind of cool. I remember really liking both these cards as soon as I saw them. In 1999 Topps commemerated the long career of Nolan Ryan (#34 of course). I'm not exactly sure why, but it was probably because he was inducted into the Hall of Fame at Cooperstown that year.
