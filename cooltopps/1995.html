1995 Topps

#216 Alex Diaz

Alex does his best Rocky Bridges here, and it is pretty good. Excelent height and extention. I hope he nailed the landing! The back of the card says that Alex is an "exciting baserunner". Ya think? In 1995 topps commemerated the 100th Birthday of The Babe with card #3 (get it). They also did some tricky photo stuff for the 2nd year in a row with #110 Walt Weiss and #67 Royce Clayton. There were other cards with this trick stuff, but the funny thing about these two is that Clayton is on both! Topps also tried some tacky stuff such as #426 Paul O'Neill. and some stuff that was a little scary (#522 Sandy Alomar).
